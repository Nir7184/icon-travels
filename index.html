Booking & Financials

  • • Initial Deposit: 50% of the total package cost is required to initiate the booking process.
  • • Final Payment: Remaining 50% must be cleared at least 15 days prior to the departure date.
  • • Pricing Policy: All rates are based on current availability and are subject to change until the deposit is received.
  • • GST & Taxes: Standard 5% GST is applicable on all domestic and international tour packages.
  • • Transaction Fees: Any credit card or payment gateway surcharges are to be borne by the customer.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • • 45+ Days Before: 10% of total tour cost will be charged as service fee.
  • • 30-44 Days Before: 25% of the total tour cost will be forfeited.
  • • 15-29 Days Before: 50% of the total tour cost will be forfeited.
  • • Less than 15 Days: 100% cancellation charges apply (No Refund).
  • • Partial Refunds: No refunds for unused services, missed meals, or early departures.

Health, Safety & Liability

  • • Medical Fitness: Travelers must be physically fit for the itinerary. High-altitude tours require a medical clearance.
  • • Accident Liability: The company is not liable for accidents, injuries, or loss of life during adventure activities.
  • • Travel Insurance: Comprehensive travel insurance is mandatory for all international and high-risk domestic tours.
  • • Personal Conduct: The company reserves the right to terminate the tour for any traveler whose conduct is disruptive.
  • • Safety Briefing: All travelers must attend and adhere to safety briefings provided by guides.

Logistics & Operations

  • • Check-in/Out: Standard hotel check-in is 14:00 and check-out is 11:00. Early check-in is subject to availability.
  • • Transport: AC vehicles will be provided as per group size. AC will not operate in hilly terrains or while parked.
  • • Itinerary Changes: The company reserves the right to modify the itinerary due to weather, road blocks, or security reasons.
  • • Force Majeure: No liability for delays caused by strikes, natural disasters, or government restrictions.
  • • Baggage: Maximum weight limit per traveler is 15kg for check-in and 7kg for cabin baggage.

Compliance & Documentation

  • • Identification: Original Government-issued photo ID (Aadhar/Passport) must be carried at all times.
  • • Permits: Inner Line Permits (ILP) or special zone permits will be processed based on the documents provided.
  • • Visa (International): Granting of Visa is at the sole discretion of the respective Embassy/Consulate.
  • • Legal Jurisdiction: All disputes are subject to the jurisdiction of courts in Mumbai, India.
  • • Privacy: Customer data is handled as per our Privacy Policy and only shared with essential service providers.

Emergency Protocols

  • • Medical Emergency: Costs for emergency evacuation or hospitalization must be paid directly by the traveler.
  • • Contact Points: 24/7 emergency numbers will be provided in the final tour voucher.
  • • Missing Items: The company is not responsible for loss of personal belongings or expensive electronics.
  • • Weather Delays: Extra costs incurred due to flight cancellations or road blocks are to be borne by the traveler.
  • • Complaints: Any service issues must be reported immediately to the ground coordinator for resolution.
